Combining geo-location tracking and mapbox api to create a MIT bus schedule app.

Ever wonder where on earth the bus is?? With an average of over 15 buses running during daytime hours, the campus at MIT has tons of stops and public transportation options to choose from. Here is an example of using free data and api's from mapbox and MIT to create a bus tracking system that updates the user every fifteen seconds as to wear on earth the busses are.
